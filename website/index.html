About the Author:

Vin Bhalerao

I am a curious, open minded, unapologetically nerdy software engineer / consultant.

I was perfecly happy writing code until I realized that writing code may be great for paying the bills, but in the real world, people who write English have a lot more fun! Who would have thought?

So finally in my 50's I worked up enough courage to start writing in English, as a hobby. At first I wrote quick little anecdotes on Facebook, then graduated to a personal blog (that no one read), and then on to Medium, where I was finally able to find some readers. After I had written about 50 blog posts, my chest filled up a little too much with pride and I mistakenly concluded that I was ready to write a whole book!

(In case you didn't know, this is known as the Dunning-Kruger effect in psychology. For more such enlightening nuggets of wisdom that will turn you into the center of attention at parties, you may want to read this book. Don't overdo it though, or you may get yourself thrown out just when things start to get interesting!)

Anyway, the rest of the story of how this book came about is included in the book itself. Happy reading!
